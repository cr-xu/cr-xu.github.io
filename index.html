About Me

I’m currently a PhD candidate at the Institute for Beam Physics and Technology (IBPT) at KIT directed by Prof. Anke-Susanne Müller, under the supervision of Andrea Santamaria Garcia.

I have given several tutorials on machine-learning related topics, which can be found here.
